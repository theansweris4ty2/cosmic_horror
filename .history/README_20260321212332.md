<h1>Cosmic Horror</h1>

<h3>Summary</h3>
<p>A horror mystery game inspired by the works of H.P. Lovecraft. Each level of the game will be connected to a Lovecraft story.</p> 
<p>You will have three characters to choose from with their own strengths and weekness. As you work your way through the level solving mysteries you will be confront with monsters and enemies from the mythos.</p>

<ul><i>Planned Mechanics</i>
<li>Side scroller</li>
<li>Obstacles</li>
<li>Enemies</li>
<li>Puzzles</li>
<li>Combat</li>
<li>Magic</li>
<li>Insanity</li>
</ul>
